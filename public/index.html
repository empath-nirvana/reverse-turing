Reverse Turing Test

An AI will ask you 3 questions.
Your job: convince it you're a machine.

In the classic Turing test, a machine tries to convince a human that it's human. Here, we flip it. An AI judge interviews two respondents — you, and another AI — and tries to figure out which one is human. Your job is to convince the judge that you're the AI.

If you fail, a machine just decided you can't think like a machine. But what does that actually prove? And if you succeed — what does it mean that you can pass as one?

Either way, you might leave wondering what the test was really measuring.
